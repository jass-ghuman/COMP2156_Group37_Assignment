Welcome to COMP 2156 - DevOps course at George Brown College! This course is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of DevOps principles and practices. From version control and continuous integration to infrastructure as code and containerization, students will explore a wide range of topics essential for modern software development. Through hands-on labs and projects, students will gain practical experience in implementing DevOps tools and techniques, preparing them for success in the rapidly evolving tech industry.
