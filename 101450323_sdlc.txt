Understanding the importance of DevOps engineers in the software development lifecycle (SDLC) is crucial for ensuring the success of any project. DevOps engineers play a vital role in automating workflows, integrating tools, and optimizing processes to accelerate development cycles and deliver high-quality software efficiently. By learning DevOps practices, students gain valuable insights into the SDLC and acquire the skills needed to bridge the gap between development, operations, and other stakeholders.
