In today's  competitive software industry, understanding the role of DevOps engineers in the software development lifecycle (SDLC) is essential for staying ahead of the curve. DevOps engineers bridge the gap between development and operations teams, enabling seamless collaboration and continuous delivery of high-quality software. By integrating DevOps practices into the SDLC, organizations can achieve faster time-to-market, improved product quality, and greater customer satisfaction.
