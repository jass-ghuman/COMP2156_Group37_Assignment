Welcome to COMP 2156 - DevOps course at George Brown College! This comprehensive course covers essential concepts and practices in DevOps, including continuous integration, continuous deployment, infrastructure as code, and more. Through hands-on projects and real-world scenarios, students gain practical experience in implementing DevOps principles to streamline software development processes and improve collaboration between development and operations teams. Join us and embark on a journey to become a skilled DevOps engineer ready to tackle the challenges of modern software development.
